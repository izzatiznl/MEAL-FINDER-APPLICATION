Fork and Feast – Interactive Meal Finder & Planner

**Fork and Feast** is an interactive meal discovery and planning application designed to bring recipes and meal ideas directly to users’ fingertips. The app provides a seamless experience to search for meals by category and ingredients, explore recipes from around the world, and organize grocery lists efficiently.

---

## Features

### Meal Discovery
- **Search by Category & Ingredients:** Users can find meals by specifying categories (e.g., Seafood, Vegetarian) or ingredients they have at home.
- **Random Meal Suggestions:** Encourages culinary exploration by providing random meal recommendations.
- **Meal Details:** View detailed information including:
  - **Meal Name**  
  - **Category and Area** (e.g., Italian, Japanese)  
  - **Ingredients and Measurements**  
  - **Step-by-step Instructions**  
  - **Images and YouTube Video Tutorials**  

### Meal Planning & Grocery Management (CRUD)
The application supports full **CRUD operations** to enhance user interaction and personalization:

1. **Create**
   - Add new grocery items with categories.
   - Plan meals for specific days in the meal planner.
2. **Read**
   - View existing grocery lists or planned meals.
3. **Update**
   - Modify saved grocery items or meal plans to adjust as needed.
4. **Delete**
   - Remove outdated items from grocery lists or meal planners to keep plans current.



## Technologies Used
- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript  
- **Framework:** Electron  
- **API:** [TheMealDB API](https://www.themealdb.com/api.php)  
- **Design Approach:** Object-Oriented JavaScript for scalable and maintainable code
